This is the core linear journey through Chemistry AI: from opening the app to searching a reaction, getting AI-generated products, reviewing the explanation, testing knowledge with a quiz, chatting further with the AI, and finally saving the session to history.
A few notes on the design choices:

Gray marks the entry and exit points (open app, save history).
Teal marks the core computational actions (search, get products, quiz).
Purple marks the AI-reasoning touchpoints (explanation, chat) — since those are where the model is actively generating and conversing rather than just computing a result.
